品牌故事
我們相信，每個人都渴望在都市中找回自然的呼吸。
薰木園 DiffuserArt 以「感官療癒」為核心，將富士山的秋楓、雪林的靜謐、沙灘的月光——凝固在多層鏤空木雕之中，佐以專業調香師手調的無酒精香薰。
藝術 × 氣味，每一件作品都是一幅可以呼吸的風景畫。
畫中有景，景中有香。

Brand Story
We believe everyone longs to reconnect with nature, even in the city.
DiffuserArt centres on sensory healing — capturing autumn leaves, snowy forests, and moonlit shores inside layered wood carvings, paired with alcohol-free scents by our perfumer.
Art × Scent. Every piece is a landscape you can breathe.
A view within the art, a scent within the view.
